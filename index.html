Swan City Roller Derby

About Us

Swan City Roller Derby is a non-profit, skater-run roller derby league in Lakeland, FL.

We play flat-track roller derby, a growing, inclusive sport where people of all backgrounds, bodies, and skill levels can find a place to belong. More than a game, derby has changed so many of our lives. It’s a space where people find confidence, friendship, and joy on wheels, and we’re deeply grateful for the warm, compassionate community that makes it all possible.

We believe roller derby is for everyone who wants to show up, put in the work, and have fun doing it. Whether you’re here to skate, volunteer, or cheer from the sidelines, you’re part of our league!
